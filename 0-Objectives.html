Objectives

  • Raising Awareness

    Even though there are tons and tons of posters, activities and protests that spreads attention towards the subject of flooding, people seem to forget and even ignore the warnings of what this disaster brings and one common factor of them ignoring is laziness. Our job is to bring attention and spread awareness in a more accessible way not only to the citizens of Cainta but also to the people that happens to stumble upon our website.

  • Information

    We focused not only on raising awareness but also on providing the necessary information that our viewers need. We're also confident on the information that you will find on our page because everything was gathered from various sources on the internet which are trusted and scientifically-proven.

  • Easy-Access Website

    Our main goal is to develop a website that is available for everyone to use and we have done just that. With the help of the lessons that our professor had taught us and with a little bit of self-learning, we can say that we have built a proper website.
